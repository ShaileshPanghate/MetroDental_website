Our Services

What We Provide

  • Root Canal

    A root canal is a dental procedure used to treat infection or damage within the pulp of a tooth.

  • Bleaching

    Bleaching is a chemical process used to lighten or remove color from materials such as fabrics, hair, or paper.

  • Teeth cleaning - Oral Prophylaxis

    It is procedure that removes plaque, tartar, and stains from the teeth to maintain oral health.

  • Laminates / Veneers

    Laminates and veneers are popular surface finishing materials used in interior design and furniture.

  • Implants

    Implants are artificial devices used to replace missing body parts or enhance bodily functions.

  • Full mouth Rehabilitation

    It is a comprehensive dental treatment that restores the function, health, and aesthetics of the entire mouth.

  • Complete Dentures

    Complete dentures are removable dental prosthetics designed to replace all missing teeth in the upper or lower jaw.

  • Othtodontic tratement

    It is focused on correcting misaligned teeth and jaw irregularities to improve oral health and aesthetics.

  • Cosmetic Dentistry

    Cosmetic dentistry focuses on enhancing the appearance of teeth, gums, and overall smile.
